Under the hood

How the proof works

1 · hash each step → 2 · chain the records → 3 · sign the session → 4 · independent notary
  • Tamper-evident: change any step and every hash after it breaks → "chain intact" means no breaks.
  • Unforgeable: signed with a private key we don't hold; verifiable by anyone with the public key.
  • Credible to third parties: a separate system signs it — not Vigilis marking its own homework.
Keep this short unless they ask. The honesty constraint matters: verifiable/auditable, never "guarantees correctness."
